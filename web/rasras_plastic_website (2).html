About Rasras Plastic

Who We Are

Since 2015, we have operated in the heart of the Egyptian market as a reliable bridge between global suppliers and manufacturers—ensuring a consistent flow of high-quality petrochemical plastic raw materials that support production and innovation.

Rasras for Commercial Agencies, Plastic Manufacturing, Import and Export is a dynamic enterprise specialized in the import and export of petrochemical plastic raw materials, serving factories and companies across various industries.

To be recognized as a leading international supplier and distributor of plastic raw materials, connecting global markets and driving industrial growth with reliability, quality, and sustainable solutions.
